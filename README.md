# The Agentic Economy

A six-part series on what happens when autonomous software agents become the primary actors in economic life.

## The Series

- `00` `The Agentic Economy` — Entry: the world today vs. the economy that is forming
- `01` `Actors Without Standing` — Identity, liability, and the accountability gap
- `02` `Judgment Without Context` — Delegation, specification, and the alignment problem
- `03` `Scale Without Organization` — Coase, the headless firm, and who controls the middle
- `04` `Risk Without Visibility` — Correlation, propagation, speed, and systemic failure
- `05` `The Hierarchy Unanchored` — Synthesis: capability without institutional anchoring
