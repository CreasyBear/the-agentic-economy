# Agentic Economy Link Building Strategy

## Objective

Earn links from sites that already shape how builders, investors, and technical operators understand AI agents, software agency, agentic commerce, authorization, and AI infrastructure.

## Linkable Assets

- Homepage: `https://agentic-economy.ai/`
- Thesis: `https://agentic-economy.ai/thesis`
- Atlas: `https://agentic-economy.ai/atlas`
- Handshake: `https://agentic-economy.ai/handshake`
- Writing index: `https://agentic-economy.ai/writing`
- Machine-readable corpus: `https://agentic-economy.ai/llms.txt`
- Full essay corpus: `https://agentic-economy.ai/llms-full.txt`
- AI discovery manifest: `https://agentic-economy.ai/ai-discovery.json`

## Priority Targets

1. Protocol and developer ecosystems
   - Agent payments, agent identity, A2A, MCP, x402, wallet, auth, and policy infrastructure projects.
   - Ask: citation from docs, ecosystem pages, example implementations, or research notes.
   - Anchor text: `agentic economy`, `agent authorization`, `AI agent infrastructure`, `software agency`.

2. Investor and market analysis publications
   - Funds, independent research desks, AI infrastructure newsletters, and market maps.
   - Ask: cite the thesis, atlas, or specific essays when discussing agentic commerce or software agency.
   - Anchor text: `The Agentic Economy`, `software agency`, `AI agents and infrastructure`.

3. Standards, governance, and risk communities
   - Identity, authorization, provenance, compliance, insurance, and operational risk groups.
   - Ask: cite the writing series as a framing resource for agent authority and institutional legibility.
   - Anchor text: `agent authorization`, `authority before action`, `AI agent risk`.

4. Founder and technical operator channels
   - Engineering blogs, founder essays, podcast notes, and implementation writeups.
   - Ask: link to Handshake as the concrete primitive and the Atlas as the field map.
   - Anchor text: `Handshake`, `agent-to-agent authorization`, `software that acts`.

## Operating Cadence

- Publish or refresh one citation-worthy asset every two weeks.
- Run ten targeted outreach messages per asset.
- Track target, contact, asset, anchor text, status, and resulting URL.
- Prioritize links that can send qualified readers, not just authority.

## Outreach Frame

Lead with the specific connection to their work, not a generic backlink request.

Example:

> I noticed your work on agent payments and authorization. We published a field map on how software agency moves through commerce, data, institutions, and physical operations. The section on authority before action may be useful context for readers thinking about agent-to-service transactions: https://agentic-economy.ai/handshake

## Measurement

- Referring domains from relevant sites.
- Links to `/thesis`, `/atlas`, `/handshake`, and individual essays.
- Mentions of `agentic economy`, `software agency`, and `agent authorization`.
- Crawls of `/llms.txt`, `/llms-full.txt`, and `/ai-discovery.json`.
